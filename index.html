About Us

Founded in 2010, MG coir mills is one of the major manufacturers of coir products in India with young and vibrant staffs. We are located at Karur the central part of Tamilnadu. At MG Coir, we produce coir piths by using high-quality raw materials from our own production facilities located in Tamil Nadu, India. What we have is a dream to provide a safer and greener future to the generations to follow by dedicating ourselves in producing high-quality products which help in enhancing the productivity with help of value pricing.

At MG coir mills, we manufacture Coir Fiber, Curled Coir rope, Coir pith, Grow bags. Strict adherence to the quality parameters has enabled us to become a standout name for premium quality Coir products. Our primary focus is to develop long term relationships with our clients and deliver high quality coir products.
